Registration Details

Download the Registration Form
Please send all registration forms to sdyc@nushigh.edu.sg

Participants may register with the school through a teacher-in-charge and have the registration sent in as a group by filling up the registration form which may be downloaded in this website or sent via email. Teachers-in-charge will need to complete the Excel sheet with the details of your student participants. Each school is strongly encouraged to send up to 30 student participants from Secondary 3, 4, JC 1 or JC 2 (or equivalent) academic years.

The payments for school registrations are to be made by cheque payable to 'NUS High School'. The registration fee per head is $85, covering materials and meals (including lunch and tea reception) for the duration of the convention. Places and allocations are subject to availability. Instructions for payment will be sent out via email once registration is confirmed.
